How access works
SLT sees all initiatives, financials, board report and can create/assign anything. Leader sees only tasks they own or assigned to their team - never SLT initiatives or financials. Member sees only tasks assigned to them. Anyone who signs in but isn't listed here is treated as a Member (lowest access).
